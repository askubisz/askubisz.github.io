Clustering algorithms comparison

Tools used: Python (scikit-learn, matplotlib, numpy, pandas)

Objective

The project aims to present a comprehensive overview of clustering algorithms, delving into both their theoretical foundations and practical applications. Since certain algorithms are better in particular situations based on their clustering methodologies, the ultimate goal is to offer practical guidance on algorithm selection within specific contexts. This work can serve as a resource for individuals seeking to gain insights and make informed choices when selecting clustering algorithms tailored to their specific requirements.

Results

Each algorithm has been examined in terms of its approach to cluster creation, the essential parameters it requires, and the heuristics for recommending the correct parameters. Furthermore, we have identified potential algorithm variations and alternative implementations that can lead to faster convergence or improved results. The summarized outcomes for each algorithm are presented in the table below.
